Exam Pattern

Subjects and Sections:

  • Physics: 35 questions
  • Chemistry: 35 questions
  • Biology: 70 questions (35 each from Botany and Zoology)

Total Questions:

  • Total Questions: 200 questions (Candidates must attempt 180 questions)

Marking Scheme:

  • Correct Answer: +4 marks
  • Incorrect Answer: -1 mark
  • Unanswered Question: 0 marks
  • Extra Attempted Question: 0 marks

Total Marks:

  • Total Marks: 720 marks

Important Notes:

  • The exam includes multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
  • The marking scheme includes negative marking for incorrect answers.
